// About Me

Background & Expertise

digital_health_ai

I am a Senior Data Scientist at Abbott, where I lead the AI/ML strategy for the Lingo bio-wearable Continuous Glucose Monitor (CGM). My work directly impacts diabetes management and metabolic health, spanning real-time glucose spike detection and GenAI-powered nutrition coaching that empower millions of users to make informed, data-driven health decisions every day.

With 18+ years in digital health, Computer Vision, and Generative AI, I hold two U.S. patents and have published peer-reviewed research on CGM manufacturing, diabetic ketoacidosis detection, and clinical data extraction. Previously at SAS Institute, I built a patented Document Vision product and deployed analytics solutions for global FMCG brands including Nestlé, Hershey’s, Kellogg’s, and Mondelez. I’m a Sigma XI member, a IICSPA Fellow, and a Raptor Fellowship recipient.
